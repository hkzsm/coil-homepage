Tokenomics

Every day, a new milestone at a certain price will be set which will act as new support. That price can't be broken. Meaning, if the support is retested sells will not go through.

Every time that support is hit, the contract will automatically trigger 0% tax on buys for 1 hours. We call that the "Happy hour".

FAQ

  • When is the new milestone set?

    Every day a new milestone (support) will be set at 6PM UTC.

  • What is it happy hour?

    Happy hour is a 1 hour period where taxes on buys will be set to 0%. Happy hour occurs whenever the previous support is retested.
